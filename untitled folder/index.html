The Lore Keeper

My expertise is at the intersection of literary skill and game logic. My **Creative Writing Bachelor's** from 2011 provides a foundation in compelling narrative structure and emotional payoff, which I apply directly to interactive media. I specialize in crafting **branching dialogue systems** and dense, internally consistent **world lore**.

With over a decade of dedicated professional experience, I bring a mature, production-focused mindset. I prioritize player agency and collaborative development.
