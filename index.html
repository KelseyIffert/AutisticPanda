The Lore Keeper

My expertise is at the intersection of literary skill and game logic. My **Creative Writing Bachelor's** provides the toolkit for structuring **compelling narrative arcs** and ensuring resonant emotional payoffs in interactive spaces. I specialize in designing branching dialogue systems and dense, internally consistent **world lore**.

With experience that spans over a decade, I bring a mature, production-focused mindset. I excel at translating core design vision into **functional script assets**, managing high-volume content, and ensuring every narrative element is precisely implemented in engines or tools like **Twine** and **Ink**. I prioritize player agency and collaborative development across disciplines.
